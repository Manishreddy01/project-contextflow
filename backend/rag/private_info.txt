Sleep is essential for physical and mental health. Adults require 7–9 hours of sleep per night
for optimal functioning.

Benefits:
- Improved memory and learning
- Better immune system performance
- Reduced stress and anxiety
- Enhanced mood and cognitive function

Sleep Stages:
1. Light sleep
2. Deep sleep
3. REM sleep (dreaming phase)

Factors Affecting Sleep Quality:
- Excessive screen time
- Irregular sleep schedule
- Caffeine intake
- Stress and poor lifestyle habits

Improving Sleep:
Maintain a consistent schedule, limit blue light exposure at night, and create a relaxing
sleep environment.
